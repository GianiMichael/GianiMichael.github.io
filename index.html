About

A bit about my path

I'm currently Global Account Lead at Six Play Consulting, where I advise a portfolio of 20+ enterprise accounts across EV, aviation, manufacturing, banking, and AI infrastructure on the technology decisions that matter most to their business. I also serve as Product Lead for ASTRA, a B2B SaaS platform I've helped build from the ground up for enterprise technology lifecycle management.

My academic background is in sustainability, and the throughline in my work has always been the same: understand a business's real constraints, translate technical complexity into a decision an executive can act on, and stay in the relationship long after the deal closes. I've advised multi-location clients on shopping their energy in deregulated markets to lower costs, and I've done extensive independent research into on-site generation, battery storage, and LEED frameworks. That's the direction I want my career to go next — helping businesses make the same kind of intelligent, well-advised decisions about their energy and sustainability future that I've spent my career helping them make about technology.
